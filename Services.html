Interim Car Service

An entry-level car service package that is designed for high-mileage drivers or those that cover 20,000 miles or more a year that may need more than one annual service. This car service includes an oil change, oil filter change and an inspection of the car's main components, such as the lights, tyres and windscreen wipers.

Full Car Service

An intermediate car service package that is usually recommended every 12 months or 12,000 miles, whichever comes first. It includes a wider range of checks and service replacement parts in addition to those carried out during an interim service.

Major Car Service

A premium car service package that is recommended every 24 months or 24,000 miles. It's the most comprehensive service package available that includes everything from a full service. In addition, parts that are recommended for replacement every two years are changed.
